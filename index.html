Sri Venkateswara College of Engineering (SVCE), a premier engineering college in Tamilnadu was established in the year 1985. The college is one of the pioneers in engineering education, excelling under the governance of Dr. A. C. Muthiah, an industrialist, cricket administrator, philanthropist, and educationist who is the Chairman of the Governing Council of the college.

Science Club

The SVCE Science Club was started by the institution with a purpose to cultivate interest among the student community to acquire scientific knowledge. Our club is an integration of three subjects, namely Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics. The door of the club is open to all the students who want to explore their interests in science. Students are provided with opportunities to engage with fellow students, professors, and eminent personalities of the field in meaningful and prudential ways.

Problem Statements
PS 1

Design and develop a product tracking system in supply chain management which is applicable for small to large scale industries.

Description:
Shipment Trackings are setting a great momentum in all the sectors of the globe. Product Tracking has become the elite spot of technology where customers are able to keep tabs on the product they purchased on online platforms. Not only online, but this has spread to every corner of the world where it has made it easy to sit in a place and check the product’s shipment progress effortlessly. Analogous to this big-hit technology, design a tracking system for an industry which does all the necessary actions for products to be imported and exported, inclusive of all the principal operations that the system must perform for achieving prime tasks.

PS 2

Smart ticket system in government buses

Description:
India is a very populated country with many people who regularly use public transport on a daily basis. A manual ticket system makes it a very tedious job to make sure everyone gets their tickets and seats properly. Design a system that displays the number of seats available, on the basis of colour. All the seats should be numbered and enabled with sensors. Once the passenger gets onto the bus, they should be able to complete the entire ticket booking process within ten minutes and an e-ticket will be sent to the passenger’s mobile. Once done, the display must get updated. If not, a warning message will be displayed, and the conductor must get notified about it after which he will come and enquire about the passenger and proceed accordingly.

PS 3

Attendance system in schools and colleges

Description:
Attendance is taken every day irrespective of the type of educational institution. Design an effective system that stores all the information about the students and is able to recognize them as they enter their classrooms. Once the stipulated time is over, anyone who enters class after that must be marked absent and the entire system must get updated daily.

PS 4

Fault indication and sensing in power cables

Description:
Design a system or model which calculates the fault location as a distance from the substation to the fault location and that information should be communicated to nearby Electricity Board station.

PS 5

Smart earth leakage detection system

Description:
To design Smart Earth Leakage Circuit Breaker (ELCB) which trips when Earth leakage is detected but it will turn on automatically after 120 second it leakage is removed or if it is temporary.

PS 6

Reduction in Transformer end cable loss from 15% to 1.0%

Description:
In load power of 4MV requirement for such a high load requirement the cable loss performs the very vital character. So the target of the project is to reduces this cable power loss from say 1.5% to 1.0%.

PS 7

System for rapid data organization as well as data retrieval by naive users without having access to confidential data in a database system

Description:
With the fast pacing technology, there is always new data emerging and it is humongously increasing because everyday new data is inserted or being deleted. For effective data management and with the shadow of security, design a system that will overcome these hassles of DBMS. Also, taking into consideration of Confidential data, modify it in a way the data can be secured from the public.

PS 8

Micro-Financing system for the public in order to restrain illegal transactions

Description:
Lack of Financial management strategies have a great impact in an individual’s life. Need for the right financial guidance and with the fast pacing tech generation, there are many financial frauds taking place and people are facing disputes. In order to circumvent this crisis, develop a modern financial methodology to improve the system.

PS 9

Low-cost smart ultrasonic water meters for water conservation

Description:
To make, develop and deploy ultrasonic water meters for monitoring water consumption in residential localities and community toilets. To conserve water by deploying the product in community toilets. To monitor and allocate drinking water and thereby save drinking water.

PS 10

Hosting a blockchain code onto a cloud network in order to create a prototype app in the Healthcare sector

Description:
Medical care is the foremost priority of all living beings and it takes a lot to make changes in this sector. Bringing up new inventions for the betterment of the world spikes up the Economy of the Nation. With the latest advancements in technology like Cloud computing and Block chain technology, develop a system that takes into account all the information required for cloud computing and setting up applications that foster an advanced healthcare system which can bring about tremendous changes in health and related sectors.

PS 11

Theft-alert systems in IoT devices for households situated in the city outskirts which link to the main security system of the nearest city

Description:
Households way off the city lines have become prone areas for thefts and these households need security forces in the right and fastest time possible. Design a smart device that will serve this need for security will be of use for many households facing this problem. An AI base system that alerts the nearby protection forces to send immediate help and can also calculate the estimated time of arrival of the forces and which can also protect homes from thieves by performing actions( for example, spraying pepper,etc.).

PS 12

Reduce wastage of lightning systems in homes, small and medium scale industries

Description:
Proper utilization of lighting not only helps wastage of power it also helps the industries to save a wholesome amount of money over a period of time. Design a system that aids in reduction of wastage of lighting systems in small and medium scale industries, and homes.

PS 13

Production of biofuels from food waste

Description:
Fuel is proportionally related to the growth of the nation. We have various fuel resources but the need for new fuels is ever increasing. Figuring out a way that can serve the purpose is essential. Food waste is one source that is generated in every household. Production of fuel using the same can come handy in terms of renewability.

PS 14

Less space consuming construction

Description:
Design or construct 1 BHK covering 400 sq ft(1 bedroom, 1 hall, 1 kitchen, 1 lavatory and 1 dining/ living room, study room ,pooja room).Designing space-saving homes does not necessarily mean to reduce the inner living spaces to their permissible minimums, but to apply several practical techniques directed to optimize and rationalize the available housing area.

PS 15

Smart waste management system

Description:
In the present-day scenario, many times we see that the garbage bins or Dust bins placed at public places in the cities are overflowing due to an increase in the waste every day. It creates an unhygienic condition for the people and creates foul smell around the surrounding that leads to the spread of some deadly diseases.

PS 16

Vehicle to vehicle communication system

Description:
A large proportion of annual deaths are due to road accidents. Design a vehicle-to-vehicle communication protocol for cooperative collision warning. It must include an effective protocol, comprising congestion control policies, service differentiation mechanisms, and methods for emergency warning dissemination. Simulation results should demonstrate that the proposed protocol achieves low latency in delivering emergency warnings and efficient bandwidth usage in stressful road scenarios.

PS 17

Artificial Intelligence in civil engineering

Description:
The ever pacing technological world has brought about a spectacular change with the introduction of Artificial Intelligence. This fresh yet futuristic technology is setting up a trademark all around the globe. It has made impeccable contributions in essential sectors of industry. Civil is the prime sector for a homo sapiens living since this sector provides the basic amenity. This sector, when collaborated with AI, can bring flourishing results into this world. Construction projects can get advanced and the prolonged process can be concised to a shorter period of time and can bring about eureka of inventions. Develop a model that ought to imply AI with Civil Engineering and demonstrate the predestined situation that can take place with this collaboration.

PS 18

Industrial effluent management and subsequent curtailment of the beneficial microbial agents

Description:
Discharged effluents from industry are responsible for deterioration of the aquatic environment in many parts of the world. Increasing industrialization and urbanization have resulted in the discharge of large amounts of waste into the environment. Design a system to utilize microbial agents such as fungi and bacteria to reduce pollutant loads such as heavy metals in effluent samples. Also, it must in a way that narrows down the toxic chemical contents of the wastewater effectively. Consequently, it must keep a tab on the chemical composition levels and it must manifest when toxicity levels reach beyond the desirable range.

PS 19

CO2 sequestration and valorization through cyanobacterial technology

Description:
We have discovered a novel fast-growing cyanobacterium and have developed a genetic engineering toolbox for leveraging it to be a sustainable cell factory for the biosynthesis of value-added compounds and biofuel precursors. In this project, we propose an efficient cyanobacterial technology that will benefit the environment by removing unwanted carbon dioxide, and the engineered bacteria can also provide sustainable energy solutions without competing with our cropland.

PS 20

Automation for hazardous jobs

Description:
Since industrialization has taken over the world in a tremendous way and is always under constant changes, yet there are the orthodox methods of the industry which are still in practice. The employees need to be aware of the rate of Danger of the particular workplace and how accordingly they can opt for the right option.

PS 21

Temperature dependent speed control appliances

Description:
Yet the discovery of many high end appliances have come to light, there is still a slight discomfort faced by users when it comes to temperature constraints. People face many kinds of discomfort when it has something to do with temperature and the right appliance with the temperature setting it right is still a dream come true.

PS 22

Simplifying the traditional pedalling mechanism in a tricycle

Description:
When it comes to cycling, the first thing that comes to mind is the way the cycle moves , THE PEDALS. The pedalling mechanism has been prevailing for centuries and yet it is considered to be one of the greatest inventions of all time which set root to the latest automobiles. What if there's a revolution in this antique mechanism? That will definitely blow minds up.That will definitely blow minds up and help in a way that disabled people can also ride tricycles and be able to commute to places.

PS 23

Optimized watering of plants

Description:
The prime source for growth of plants is sunlight and water. Now since sunlight is optimally used by the plants by mechanisms and biological cycles, the main drawback is for water. Since the majority of the plants depend on water for their growth, it is highly essential for plants to get the right amount of water because both the extreme levels of water can be drastic and affect the plants.

PS 24

Navigation system for the Visually Impaired

Description:
Navigation systems have become a major part in all the sectors of the society. Well in the case of a visually impaired person, they face many challenges in transportation and hence they need to also be assisted.

PS 25

Narcotics Detector

Description:
Handheld contraband tracing devices with use of microcontrollers for determining the presence as well as quantity of supposed contraband articles. This will be a hardware device which will be able to identify and trace the drug/narcotics substance even if hidden in bag/suitcase. Also, this hardware device must be able to connect its scanning.

F A Q

Frequently Asked Questions

Here we address some of the most frequently asked queries of the participants regarding the event. Please go through this section to get an epitomized understanding of everything you need to know.

01. What is the criteria for getting shortlisted?

Participants will be shortlisted for the preliminary round under each category based on the ABSTRACT submitted.

02. How final round will be?

Based on the presentation, participants will be shortlisted for the final round where they need to present their complete work in front of the panel of Judges.

03. What can be the team size?

Students can form a team of 2 to 4, with participants pursuing any engineering stream, studying in any year.

04. Can a participate be in more than 1 team?

A participant cannot be a part of more than 1 team and each team can work only on 1 problem statement.

05. What type of solutions are allowed?

All types of solutions including Hardware, Software, Hypothesis Design and Prototype are allowed.

06. Will participation certificates be provided?

Yes. Participation certificates will be provided to all the participants.

07. How much is the cash prize for top performers?

Cash prizes worth 50K INR for top performers.

08. Will the participants get any Internship offers?

Yes, Internship offers are provided for TOP PERFORMERS.

09. Is this event beginner friendly?

Yes, this event is beginner friendly.

10. Is there any registration fee?

No. There is no registration fee.
